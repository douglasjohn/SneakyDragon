Incorporating driver expectations into a taxonomy of transfers of control for automated vehicles

My very first research paper and primary introduction to the field of Human-Computer Interaction, this paper is a major reason why I want to be a scientist. This paper adresses the many ways to transfer control of the driving task between Human and Automated Vehicle (AV). As AV technology becomes widely adapted, it's important for researchers to use the same terminology when studying a certain type of transfer of control.

  • Am I overriding or is the vehicle taking control?
  • What happens if the car initiates a transfer of control?
